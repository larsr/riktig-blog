The equivalences catalog

Every proven equation and logical iff in riktig’s Coq development — the rewrite rules — one file, grouped by theory, each with a link to the exact line where it’s proved. Compiled 2026-07-08.

Scope. An entry here is a theorem whose conclusion is lhs == rhs (the Riktig.Ops setoid), lhs = rhs (plain Leibniz equality, used by the Channel/ and Lambda/ tracks, which don’t carry the algebra setoid), or P <-> Q. Pure inequalities (a <= b), existence theorems, and relation-valued conclusions (subseq r s, is_deriv f a L) are out of scope — they’re catalogued by the roadmap docs instead, not here. Interface law fields (the axioms a class states, not derived facts) are marked “(interface law)”; everything else is a proved theorem.

How to read an entry. Each bullet gives the Coq name, the statement rendered as math, and a link to file.v#Lline on git.ri.se. Hypotheses (if any) precede the equation.
